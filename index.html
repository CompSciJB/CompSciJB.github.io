A little about my education

For the last several years I have dedicated my life to the pursuit of two separate bachelor degrees at the University of Arizona while working in several internships for a greater portion of it. When talking about Computer Science, I can look back and see several years of trails that have taken me through some of the hardest challenges I have ever faced, but coming out on top I have learned that I have the foundation to make it far in this career as well as the fact that I will never stop learning while in this career field. There will always be new puzzles to solve and software to build and I am incredibly excited to be a part of a field that will never cease to expand.

It has not only opened my eyes to the difficulties of what it is like to learn a foreign language, but it has also made a more understanding and compassionate person as well as a better communicator.
